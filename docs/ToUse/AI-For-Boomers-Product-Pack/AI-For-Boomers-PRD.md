# AI for Boomers – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Purpose
Formal definition of requirements to deliver AI for Boomers as a production-grade product.

## Goals
- Reliability ≥ 99.9%
- High caregiver trust
- Subscription-ready

## Functional Scope
- Voice-first assistant
- Calendar, meds, contacts, notes
- Notifications with escalation
- Social sharing (private-first)
- Caregiver portal

## Non-Functional
- Accessibility (WCAG AA+)
- Privacy by default
- Observability & auditability
