# Regulatory Readiness Memo

AI for Boomers is positioned as a personal organization and reminder platform.

## Scope
- No diagnosis
- No treatment
- No clinical decision-making

## Safeguards
- Explicit user consent
- No public sharing of health data
- Audit logs and access controls

## HIPAA Position
Healthcare-adjacent, not a Covered Entity.
Architecture supports HIPAA alignment if required by partners.
